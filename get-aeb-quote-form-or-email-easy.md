For an AEB Quote Proposal, simply submit a form at your [AEB dashboard](https://aeblogistics.com/my-account/quotesform/). Your AEB account manager will work on your request and inform you once a quote is ready.

Or you may email us at support@aeblogistics.com with shipment details as follows. A ticket will be created for your [AEB](https://www.trustpilot.com/review/aeblogistics.com) account manager to send quote to you. Please as well check your spam folder as in some cases emails from support@aeblogistics.com may be put to spam.

1. 1. Origin
    2. Destination
    3. Freight incoterms – FOB or EXW
    4. Cargo HS code and cargo value for an estimation on import duties and taxes
    5. Box count/cbm/kgs
    6. Estimated date of cargo readiness at origin
    7. Optional/Comments: Please share target price for streamlined quoting, reaching satisfactory shipping solution for both parties.
